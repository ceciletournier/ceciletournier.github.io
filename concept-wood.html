CONCEPT WOOD

#BrandIdentity #Ux #Ui

CONTEXT AND BRIEF

This project had been realized during my internship in Studio Pantone, a small web and print communication agency. Here is a proposition I have made for a client. And also the project I choose for the final jury at school to graduate my BA. Concept wood is a independent company specialized in parquet and carpentry. It offers a range of quality services to its customers with higher incomes. For this project I was in charge of creating a brand identity, designing business cards and a website, and coding (only html and css) the website.

THE PROJECT

After realizing a competition benchmark and establishing the strategy for the brand, I created a visual identity that could represent the best the brand. It has a rather upscale positioning, and offers products with quality materials so it was important to highlight them. I chose a very contrasting environment of white and black to emphasize the materials and colors of the wood.
