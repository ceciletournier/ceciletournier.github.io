LA LANGUE VIVANTE

#BrandIdentity #Ux #Ui #MobileApp

CONTEXT AND BRIEF

This is a school project, that I've made at ECV Digital Paris. New entrepreneur came to school to talk with us and present their buisness. We have to choose two buisness and propose them brand identity and two page of the future website or app. The second project I've choosen was a food blogger who wanted to share in an other way than instagram the good places she discovers.

THE PROJECT

I created a mobile app where all the places she tries are classified. You can search with filters, on a list, on a map and also with a feed of pictures which I think is the most important one because pictures give us the desire to try a new restaurant. I created a really colorful, young and fun identity for her because it is exactly what fits her.
