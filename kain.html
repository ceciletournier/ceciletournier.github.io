KAIN - HAND DYE BATIK

#BrandIdentity #Ux #Ui

CONTEXT AND BRIEF

This is a school project I've made during my first year of Master Degree. New entrepreneurs came to school to present us their buisness. We had to choose two buisness and suggest them a new brand identity and at least two pages of a future website or mobile app. The first one I've choosen was an online shop selling fabric and handmade cushion in Indonesia. The brand is premium, invested in ecology and promote ancestral heritage.

THE PROJECT

After realizing the brand strategy, I created the brand identity. I decided to make a proposal for a new name, because I was thinking that "Indowpillow" was too restrictive; I wanted to find something better. I decided to pick "Kain", because it allows more evolution about selling different types of products. It also permits the company to go international, because it does not mean anything in other countries except Indonesia where it means fabric. Then, I created a responsive e-shop, that fit the most with the company's positionning and highlighting the unique handmade fabric.
